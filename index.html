Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care

From emergency treatments to routine care, we provide complete dental solutions for the whole family in North York.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional dental hygiene to prevent gum disease, remove plaque, and maintain optimal oral health.

Clear Aligners

Invisible orthodontic treatment to straighten teeth discreetly without traditional metal braces.

Dental Fillings

Tooth-colored composite fillings that restore decayed teeth naturally and blend seamlessly.

Dentures

Custom-fitted partial and full dentures that restore your smile and chewing function naturally.

Tooth Extractions

Gentle removal of damaged or impacted teeth including wisdom teeth surgery with minimal discomfort.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions that look, feel, and function like your natural teeth.

Dental Braces

Traditional and ceramic braces for complex orthodontic correction and bite alignment.

Root Canal Therapy

Pain-free treatment to save infected teeth, eliminate bacteria, and preserve your natural smile.
